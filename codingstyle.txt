This EnAccess library uses Artistic Style for code formatting:
http://astyle.sourceforge.net/

The astyle options are the following:
--style=kr
--indent=spaces=4
--indent-after-parens
--indent-modifiers 
--align-pointer=type
--align-reference=type 
--convert-tabs
--attach-namespaces
--max-code-length=100
--pad-header
